Niki Manoledaki is a [Senior Software Engineer](https://www.linkedin.com/in/niki-manoledaki-9b505111b/) currently on the Platform team at [Grafana Labs](https://grafana.com/), focused on helping developers and businesses monitor and cut costs in the agentic era of cloud computing. She is a CNCF Ambassador and leader in open source software in domains such as platform engineering, cloud sustainability, and operational efficiency. She worked with IBM and Red Hat to donate [Kepler](https://github.com/sustainable-computing-io/kepler/issues), a Prometheus exporter for energy monitoring in Kubernetes, to the CNCF. She is a [CNCF Ambassador](https://www.cncf.io/people/ambassadors/), [Green Software Foundation](https://greensoftware.foundation/) Champion, and an organizer of the [Cloud Native Barcelona](https://www.meetup.com/cloud-native-barcelona/) meetup. Previously she has presented a keynote talk at KubeCon + CloudNativeCon and chaired the conference's Platform Engineering track.
