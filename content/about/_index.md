Niki Manoledaki is a [Senior Software Engineer](https://www.linkedin.com/in/niki-manoledaki-9b505111b/) currently on the Platform team at [Grafana Labs](https://grafana.com/), focused on helping developers and businesses monitor and cut costs in the agentic era of cloud computing. Her technical expertise is in cloud sustainability and operational efficiency in Kubernetes and cloud environments. She is a leader in open source software as a [CNCF Ambassador](https://www.cncf.io/people/ambassadors/), a [Green Software Foundation](https://greensoftware.foundation/) Champion, and an organizer of the [Cloud Native Barcelona](https://www.meetup.com/cloud-native-barcelona/) meetup. In the past, she has presented a keynote at KubeCon + CloudNativeCon and chaired the conference's Platform Engineering track. She also collaborated with IBM and Red Hat to donate [Kepler](https://github.com/sustainable-computing-io/kepler/issues), a Prometheus exporter for energy monitoring in Kubernetes, to the CNCF.
